Formatting Guide

Time:

StringResult
hhHour in 12hr format (e.g. 12)
HHHour in 24hr format (e.g. 13)
NOTE: You must turn on turn on 24hr time in Settings > General > Date & Time
mmMinutes (e.g. 56)
ssSeconds (e.g. 45)
aam / pm
Example: hh:mm:ss a12:45:33 pm

Day:

StringResult
ddThe numeric day (e.g. 31)
EThe short day (e.g. Tue)
EEEEThe full day (e.g. Tuesday
EEEEEThe day intial (e.g. T)

Month:

StringResult
MMThe numeric month (e.g. 12)
MMMThe short month (e.g. Dec)
MMMMThe full month (e.g. December)
MMMMMThe month inital (e.g. D)

Year:

StringResult
yyThe short numeric year (e.g. 14)
yyyyThe long numeric year (e.g. 2014)

Custom text:

StringResult
'doge'Any string between '' (e.g. doge)
